About College

SJB Institute of Technology is one amongst these institutions started during the year 2001. SJBIT is committed to personal, social, and intellectual growth emphasizing a comprehensive educational programme leading to high academic standards. Spread across sprawling 50 acres of land, the SJBIT is located amidst natural serene atmosphere, 14 km from Bengaluru city, near Kengeri. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

About Fest

The first Techno-Cultural edition of the Echora fest scaled unparalleled grandeur and garnered stern response for the Eco-Marathon sponsored by BGS Global Hospitals. Besides, the three-day long event hosted the IEEE Conference with nearly 100 jury members of foreign delegation and witnessed over 4000 participants.
However, ECHORA 2k18 will see The Karnataka Air Pollution Board tie up with the BGS Global Hospitals to subsist the Eco-Marathon 2.0. The grand inauguration will be staged by Roaring Star SRI MURULI. ECHORA 2k18 will be based exclusively on an all-filmy theme which is all set to astound the audience.
