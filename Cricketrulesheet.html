Rule Sheet

Rules

  1. Team consists of 6+1 players.
  2. 4 overs match with 4 balls per over.
  3. 2-run for wide, 2-run for no-ball without freehit.
  4. Ball should be delivered without bending knee and without lifting the heel.
  5. Only byes apply and no leg byes.
  6. Boundary Out even for no ball. No Single batting.
  7. Overthrows are applicable.
  8. Umpire’s decision is final.Arguing with umpires’ decision and creating nuisance will result in cancellation of entry without refund.
  9. Re-entry is applicable only for prelims. All matches are knockouts.
  10. College ID card is mandatory.
